About Sheth M J High School

Sheth M J High School, established in 1917, stands as a beacon of educational excellence in Viramgam, Gujarat. For over a century, our institution has been dedicated to providing quality education and shaping the minds of future generations.

Our Rich History

Founded under the Charitable Endowments Act-1890, our school began its journey with the noble vision of making education accessible to all. Originally established as Viramgam English School, it was later renamed to honor the contributions of the Sheth family to education in our region.

The school was initially founded with support from the local community and philanthropists who believed in the power of education. Over the decades, we have evolved from a small local school to a comprehensive educational institution serving hundreds of students.

Vision

Mission

To provide quality education in a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence, creativity, ethical values, and holistic development of every student.

School Infrastructure

Our campus features modern facilities designed to support comprehensive education:

  • 23 well-equipped classrooms in excellent condition
  • Dedicated computer laboratory with 15 functional computers
  • Library housing over 12,776 books
  • Separate facilities for boys (6 toilets) and girls (3 toilets)
  • Playground for sports and recreational activities
  • Separate room for Head Teacher/Principal
  • Additional rooms for non-teaching activities
  • Reliable electricity and water supply
