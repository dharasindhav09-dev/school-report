About Us

Sheth M J High School, established in 1917, is dedicated to excellence in education. With a strong academic curriculum, innovative teaching, and emphasis on extracurricular activities, the school ensures the all-round development of students.

Vision

To inspire students to become lifelong learners, critical thinkers, and responsible global citizens.

Mission

To provide quality education in a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence, creativity, and ethical values.
