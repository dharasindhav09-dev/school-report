Academic Excellence

Sheth M J High School offers comprehensive education from Grade 1 to Grade 12, following the Gujarat State Education Board (GSEB) curriculum. Our academic program is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in core subjects while encouraging critical thinking and practical application of knowledge.

School Details

Sheth M J High School was established in 1917 and is managed as a Grant In Aided institution. Located in the urban area of Viramgam block, Ahmedabad district, Gujarat, our school has been serving the educational needs of the community for over a century.

Key Features:

  • Grades Offered: 1st to 12th Standard
  • Medium of Instruction: Gujarati
  • Co-educational Institution
  • Board Affiliation: Gujarat State Education Board (GSEB)
  • School Type: Grant In Aided
  • Location: Urban area with all-weather road access

Curriculum Structure

Secondary Section (Grades 9-10)

  • Gujarati Language and Literature
  • English Language and Literature
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Social Studies
  • Sanskrit, Hindi (Optional for 10 th)

Higher Secondary Section (Grades 11-12)

  • Commerce Stream: Accountancy, Statestics, B.A., S.P., Economics
  • Arts Stream: Hindi, Sanskrit, Philosophy, Sociology, S.P.
  • Compulsory Subjects: Gujarati, English

Teaching Methodology

Our experienced faculty employs modern teaching methods including:

  • Interactive classroom sessions
  • Computer-aided learning
  • Group discussions and presentations
  • Regular assessments and feedback
  • Individual attention to students
