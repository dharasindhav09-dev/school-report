Admissions Information

Welcome to Sheth M J High School admissions! We are committed to providing quality education and welcome applications from students who are eager to learn and grow in our nurturing academic environment.

Admission Process

Admission forms are available at the school office during working hours or can be downloaded from our website. Admission is based on merit, availability of seats, and adherence to state government guidelines.

Grades Available for Admission

  • Grade 9 (Standard 9)
  • Grade 10 (Standard 10)
  • Grade 11 (Standard 11) - Science, Commerce, Arts streams
  • Grade 12 (Standard 12) - Science, Commerce, Arts streams

Admission Requirements

  • Completed admission application form
  • Original birth certificate and photocopy
  • Previous school leaving certificate (TC)
  • Mark sheets of previous qualifying examination
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)
  • Income certificate (for fee concession, if applicable)
  • Passport-size photographs (4 copies)
  • Aadhar card copy of student and parents
  • Medical fitness certificate

Important Dates

Admission Process Timeline:

  • Admission forms available: March 1st
  • Last date to submit application: April 30th
  • Merit list publication: May 15th
  • Document verification: May 20th - May 25th
  • Fee payment deadline: May 30th
  • Academic session begins: First week of June

Fee Structure

As a private aided school, we maintain affordable fee structure:

  • Admission Fee: ₹500 (one-time)
  • Annual Tuition Fee: ₹2,400 (payable in installments)
  • Development Fee: ₹300 (annual)
  • Library Fee: ₹100 (annual)
  • Sports Fee: ₹200 (annual)
  • Examination Fee: ₹150 (annual)

Note: Fee structure is subject to change as per government guidelines. Concessions available for economically weaker sections.
