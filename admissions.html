Admissions Information

Welcome to Sheth M J High School admissions! We are committed to providing quality education and welcome applications from students who are eager to learn and grow in our nurturing academic environment.

Admission Process

Admission forms are available at the school office during working hours or can be downloaded from our website. Admission is based on merit, availability of seats, and adherence to state government guidelines.

Grades Available for Admission

  • Grade 9 (Standard 9)
  • Grade 10 (Standard 10)
  • Grade 11 (Standard 11) - Commerce, Arts streams
  • Grade 12 (Standard 12) - Commerce, Arts streams

Admission Requirements

  • Completed admission application form
  • Original birth certificate and photocopy
  • Previous school leaving certificate (TC)
  • Mark sheets of previous qualifying examination
  • Student Caste certificate
  • Parent Income certificate
  • Passport-size photographs (2 copies)
  • Aadhar card copy of student and parents
  • Student Bank Passbook Xerox
